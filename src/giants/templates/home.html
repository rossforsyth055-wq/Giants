Meet Our Trainers

Trainer Photo

David Low

With over 25 years of experience in the fitness industry, I am a qualified Personal Trainer with a strong passion for health, movement, and helping people achieve their potential. Throughout my career I have worked in a variety of roles including lifeguard, kickboxing instructor, and kids' sports coach, giving me the opportunity to support individuals of all ages and abilities.

I believe fitness should be accessible, enjoyable, and sustainable, and I'm committed to helping people build confidence, strength, and healthier lifestyles.

Outside of work, staying active is a big part of my life. Whether training in the gym, cycling, or climbing Munros, I enjoy pushing myself and spending time outdoors — bringing that same energy and enthusiasm to the people I work with.

Lyndsay

I am a Personal Trainer and Fitness Instructor specialising in women's health and strength training. I am currently studying for a BSc (Hons) in Physical Activity and Health while completing my Level 3 qualification in Working with People with Long-Term Conditions, with a particular interest in diabetes and exercise management.

I am passionate about providing evidence-based guidance that supports sustainable, long-term health and fitness. Whether you are new to the gym and looking to build confidence with strength training, or already enjoy lifting and want to improve your physical or mental wellbeing, I tailor sessions to help you achieve your goals safely and effectively.

Trainer Photo

Martin

I am a Massage Therapist and Personal Trainer specialising in injury management, pain reduction, and improving mobility. My approach combines deep tissue, Thai, holistic, and soft tissue massage with assisted stretching and targeted strengthening to support recovery and better movement.

Each session is tailored to address the root cause of discomfort, helping clients restore balance in the body, reduce pain, and improve long-term mobility. Whether recovering from injury, managing chronic pain, or simply looking to move better, I aim to provide effective, personalised support at every stage of the journey.

I am currently studying Sports Rehabilitation to further develop my knowledge and provide evidence-based care for my clients.
